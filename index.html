Technical Games Production

A bullet hell game is a game that bombardes the player with endless waves of bullets for the player to dodge. Some bullet hell's (such as Binding Of Isaac) may not be endless but feature multiple rooms for the player to make their way through. The game that I made features endless waves.

This game was made during the first 6 weeks of the second year of university in the TGP (Technical Games Production) module, and was a project that I made by myself. It was made using Unreal Engine 4 with mostly C++ and some blueprints.

The game features 4 weapons to choose from; a single shot rifle, a triple barrel shotgun, a grenade launcher and rocket launcher. Each weapon gives the player a unique playstyle as they have different effects. For example, the shotgun pushes the player back a little so they have to be wary of what's behind them.

Tower Defence
